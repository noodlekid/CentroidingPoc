# ADR-0000: Choosing a Decision Template

- **Date:** 2026-04-14
- **Status:** Accepted
- **Owner:** Mateusz Tyc

## Context
We need a standardized template for documenting architectural decisions in our project. This template should be clear, concise, and provide all necessary information to understand the decision, its rationale, and its consequences. The template will help ensure consistency across all architectural decision records (ADRs) and facilitate better communication among team members and stakeholders.

## Decision
I've decided to adopt the template you are seeing now.

- **Positive:** Minimal, and low friction.
